Dragons are the main antagonists in the game. To win, you must beat two elemental dragons and then the elder dragon. Here's some fun art of the elder dragon that we found online!

There are four different elemental dragons, each of which have different stats and give the player a different boost:

  • Inferno Dragon: Attack and crit. Upon beating it, it gives boosts your attack and gives your attacks a chance to burn.
  • Ocean Dragon: Healing. Upon beating it, you heal a bit of health every turn.
  • Cloud Dragon: Speed. Upon beating it, your attacks have a chance to strike twice.
  • Mountain Dragon: Defense. Upon beating it, your blocks mitigate more damage.
