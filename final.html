  • Project Description

    This project would be a text-based RPG-esque game, where the player would be presented with options and has choices on what they should do. Should they fight, run, or heal? What items should you use? Every room you will have a random encounter for your phase of the game. When you beat them, you will gain a certain amount of experience. As you gain more experience and level up, you grow stronger as time goes on. When you feel ready, fight. Every time you beat a dragon, you propel your world further and gain buffs for your character. Stronger enemies will spawn and you will gain more gold. Face off against the final dragon in an epic battle, saving the world.

  • Instructions

    To start playing the game, run the main.py file on your computer. Make sure that your computer has python installed. Python 3.9 is recommended. While in battle, you can follow the prompts to fight. Input a number to do the action that it corresponds to. You can also use !stats to view the stats of either you or your opponent. Have fun!

  • How it works

    The game works by progressing rooms. In every room there is a chance that there is an ambient message (random choice by splitting a txt file), a healing zone, a treasure chest, or an enemy (random choice by splitting a csv file). Defeating an enemy grants you exp. With a certain amount of exp, you level up and become stronger. Every 25 rooms, you will have the option to challenge a dragon. Upon beating a dragon, you will gain certain combat boosts and propel your world into the next phase. Every new phase brings new enemies that are split from the aforementioned csv file. This csv file also contain their stats, which we randomize a bit upon picking it. When you spawn a dragon we also choose one randomly from this csv file and their stats are also included. The final dragon that you fight is always the Elder Dragon, which upon beating ends the game. Dying at any time also ends the game. Your stats are kept in a dictionary which we edit when you level up or use certain potions (random chance from treasure chests). Your base stats and stats upon leveling up are based on the class you choose at the beginning of the game. Upon recieving a potion, you put it in an inventory which is kept in a list. Your dragon combat boosts are also kept in a list.
